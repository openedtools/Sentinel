💡 Sec+ Key Concept

An IOC (Indicator of Compromise) is forensic evidence that a system may have been breached — IP addresses, file hashes, malicious domains, registry keys.

Threat Intelligence is context that makes IOCs actionable: who is attacking, with what tools, for what motive. Feeds like VirusTotal, GreyNoise, and MISP automate this enrichment.
