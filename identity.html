💡 IAM Key Concepts

Least Privilege — users should have only the minimum permissions needed for their role. Extra permissions = extra attack surface.

PAM (Privileged Access Management) — controls, monitors, and audits all privileged account usage. Domain Admin access should be just-in-time, not permanent.

MFA stops 99.9% of automated credential attacks. Admin accounts without MFA are the highest-value target for attackers.
