💡 Sec+ Key Concept

Asset criticality drives risk impact scores. A Domain Controller breach is Critical because it controls authentication for every user. An unpatched workstation is High if it's used by a privileged admin.

CIA Triad: Criticality reflects which component matters most — Confidentiality (PII data), Integrity (domain controllers), or Availability (production servers).
