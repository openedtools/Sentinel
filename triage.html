💡 Key Concept

A real SOC receives 50,000+ alerts per day. AI-enabled SIEMs reduce this to a manageable ~200 prioritized incidents — a 99%+ noise reduction.

Without AI assistance, analysts experience alert fatigue — missing real threats buried in false positives.

Classification Guide
TP True Positive

Confirmed malicious activity. Requires response.

FP False Positive

Benign activity that triggered an alert. Safe to dismiss.

ESC Escalate

Ambiguous — needs human analyst review before acting.
